Rules

This Rules are made for guide to all users to avoid any type of harassment, if your ban differs with the following reasons, let us tell you that you have the right to appeal and get unbanned for free.
RULES MAY BE ADDED OR CHANGED AT ANY TIME, IT IS UP TO THE PLAYERS TO MAKE SURE THEY KEEP UP TO DATE ON THESE.

General Information and Rules:

  • Regular bans: Regular bans can be appealed via our ticket system. We do not do ban appeals over teamspeak.
  • Mutes: Only permanent mutes can be appealed via our ticket system.
  • Blacklists: Cannot be appealed. If you were blacklisted it means we are not interested in having you in the network anymore. Again, they cannot be appealed.

Hacked Clients / Cheating:

  • First Offense: Permanent ( Admitted 6 Months )
  • Proceeding Offenses : Permanent.
    • Someone famous in Source Title

AutoClick / Macros:

  • First Offense: Permanent ( Admitted 30 Days )
  • Second Offenses : Permanent.
    • Modifying your mouse in ANYWAY Is not allowed

Ban Evading:

  • First Offense: Permanent.
    • If you wanna keep playing on the network just BUY AN UNBAN. If you already buy an unban and don't get it, wait until an Mod get on for support you.

DDoSing / DoSing / Doxing:

  • First Offense: BlackList
    • These are considered federal crimes in most countries and will NOT be tolerated on our servers. If you have any evidence of somebody committing these acts, please show us through TeamSpeak. This includes ALL DDoS threats, joke or not.

ChargeBack:

  • First Offense: BlackList
    • For Appeal this Ban Reason you should talk with an Admin/Owner

Advertising of another Server / Server Disrespect:

  • First Offense: Warning
  • Second Offense: Kick
  • Third Offense: 7 Days Ban
    • Advertising or Server Disrespect any other server IS NOT ALLOWED.

Death Threaths / Suicide Encouragement / Suicide jokes:

  • First Offense: 3 Days Ban
    • No need to joke about commit suicide. We try to maintain a casual atmosphere in chat.

Practice Information and Rules:
